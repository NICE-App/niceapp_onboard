Privacy & Policy

Thank you for using NICE APP, the internal communication platform exclusively for employees of NICE. This Privacy Policy outlines how we handle your information when you use NICE APP.


Information We Collect:

Authentication Data: When you log in to NICE APP, we collect authentication data provided by your NICE email account. This may include your email address and associated metadata.

Communication Data: We collect data related to your interactions within NICE APP, including messages, posts, files, and any other content you create or share. Device Information: We may collect information about the devices you use to access NICE APP, including device identifiers, IP addresses, and browser type.

How We Use Your Information: Internal Communication: We use your information to facilitate communication among employees of NICE within NICE APP.

Account Management: Your information is used for account authentication and management purposes.

Improvement of Services: We may analyze usage patterns to improve NICE APP and enhance user experience.

Data Sharing: Within NICE: Your information is shared within NICE to enable internal communication and collaboration.

Data Security: We employ industry-standard security measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Access to your information within NICE APP is restricted to authorized personnel who require it for business purposes.

Data Retention: We retain your information for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this Privacy Policy, unless a longer retention period is required by law.

Your Rights: Access and Correction: You have the right to access and update your personal information stored in NICE APP.

Data Portability: Upon request, we will provide you with a copy of your personal data in a commonly used, machine-readable format. Withdrawal of Consent: You may withdraw your consent for the processing of your personal information at any time, subject to legal or contractual restrictions.

Updates to this Privacy Policy: We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. Any updates will be communicated within NICE APP.

Contact Us: If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or the handling of your personal information, please contact us at contact@niceapp.ng.

By using NICE APP, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. Please review this policy periodically for any updates or changes.
